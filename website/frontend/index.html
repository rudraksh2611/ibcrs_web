About IBCRS

Intelligent BioTech Component Recognition System

What is IBCRS?

IBCRS is a state-of-the-art AI-powered system designed to automatically identify and classify biotech laboratory equipment through real-time video analysis. It leverages advanced deep learning models (YOLOv8) to provide accurate, fast, and reliable component detection.

Our Journey

Founded in 2025, IBCRS was created to address a critical challenge facing modern laboratories: efficient equipment inventory management and rapid component identification. What started as a university research project has evolved into a powerful tool trusted by biotech facilities worldwide.
